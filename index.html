About NETPRO

A decade of steel, service and skilled hands

Ngimzwile Engbiza Trading & Projects (Pty) Ltd — known as NETPRO — was founded in 2016 in Richards Bay as an events management firm. It was soon joined by qualified, experienced statutory and divisional artisans bringing more than ten years in the engineering field and a clear understanding of the challenges facing the engineering fraternity.

Today NETPRO provides professional mechanical and electrical engineering consulting to the public sector, private sector and state-owned enterprises, with expertise drawn from smelters, production plants, mines, storage facilities and chemical plants.

NETPRO is a Level 1 B-BBBEE contributor, 100% Black-owned and 67% Black Female-owned — spearheaded by some of the most experienced artisans in the industry.

Managing Director

Ndumiso Mdamba
